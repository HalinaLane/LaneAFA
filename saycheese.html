My Journey in CheeseTart Business

"Say Cheese Tart" and "Almond Choco Tart"

    BEGINNING

    My business began in 2020 as a result of the covid19 epidemic.My interest in the cheese tart company began when I noticed others who enjoyed cheese tarts.

    It was the primary reason I began selling with close friends.

    Strawberry Cheese Tart, Blueberry Cheese Tart and Nutella Cheese Tart is one of three basic sorts of seasonings that I produce.

    My business transaction proceeded easily and at a price that I could afford as a student.

    My lovely family is really supportive of me in this endeavour.Grateful to Allah SWT, for everything happened as planned.

    IMPROVEMENT

    High demand from new and returning customers has resulted in the creation of new items with product innovation, such as Almond Choco Tart.

    In fact, I registered the company in early 2021 using the name Lane Z Resources as my business name. This new product is more flexible and long-lasting. Because it is made with premium chocolate, high-quality almonds, and a harder tart shell, it may be eaten at any time and brought anywhere.

    I had set a high sales target for the holiday season, and it was met. May this venture be a more positive turning point in my life.
